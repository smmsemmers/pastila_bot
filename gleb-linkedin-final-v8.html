About

I run a fruit-snack and confectionery manufacturing business in Riga, Latvia — premium pastila, meringues and ice cream — exporting to 30+ markets across the EU, Balkans and Scandinavia, with ~100 active B2B accounts and an active market-entry pipeline in Japan and North America.

Owner-operator, hands-on across the full cycle: sales and key accounts, production planning, procurement, and trade compliance — from TARIC classification and preferential origin to a customs advance ruling with Japan Customs under the EU-Japan EPA.

I've built private-label projects for EU retail, taken products through supplier onboarding, labeling and claims compliance, and turned production offcuts into a standalone high-margin SKU. Currently automating SME food operations with LLM tools and computer vision.

Pastila, if you haven't met it: premium fruit-based confectionery with meringue structure — naturally low-fat, and a genuinely underrated export category.

Available for expert consultations on: confectionery & snacks route-to-market and distribution, private label and EU retail entry, food trade compliance (TARIC and HS classification, rules of origin, landed cost, EU-Japan EPA advance ruling), and Russia/CIS consumer-market insights at a category level.
